Frequently Asked Questions

The most commonly asked questions from new players

What kind of game is LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight?
An action-adventure LEGO game developed by TT Games and published by Warner Bros. This entry introduces, for the first time in the series, an Arkham-style freeflow combat system (attack combos, counters, Focus Takedowns), Predator stealth mechanics, and a fully open-world Gotham City — all firsts for LEGO Batman. Main story: 12–15 hours. 100% completion: ~35 hours.
What platforms is it on? When does it release?
PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC (Steam & Epic Games Store) — Standard Edition: May 29, 2026. The Deluxe Edition includes 72-hour early access (playable from May 26). A Nintendo Switch 2 version is coming later in 2026.
What's the difference between Standard and Deluxe Edition?
Standard Edition ($69.99): Full main story + pre-order bonus (The Dark Knight Returns suit). Deluxe Edition ($89.99): All Standard content + Legacy Collection at launch + Mayhem Collection coming September 2026 (playable Joker & Harley Quinn with new story missions) + Golden Age suit pre-order bonus. Deluxe also includes 72-hour early access.
Can I go back for missed collectibles?
Yes. Free Play Mode unlocks after completing the story — replay any mission via the Batcomputer with any unlocked character. You can also replay completed missions during the main story to grab missed items. All collectible progress is retained across replays.
Is there online multiplayer?
2-player local drop-in/drop-out co-op — Player 2 can join or leave at any time. No online multiplayer. No split-screen competitive mode.
How do Stud multiplier Red Bricks work?
They are multiplicative, not additive. x2 + x4 = x8 (not x6). All five multipliers (x2, x4, x6, x8, x10) active = x3,840 total multiplier. One Blue Stud (base 1,000) becomes 3,840,000 studs. This is the single most important economic system in the game.
What unlocks after beating the game?
Post-game unlocks include: Free Play Mode, full character roster (all villains playable), Batcave Challenge Board, Bat-Mite complete shop, Dark Knight difficulty, and all Batcave expansion terminals. 100% completion requires all Red Bricks, WayneTech Caches, Batcave Trophies, and Minikit vehicle models.
