Boss Guide

Weakness analysis, phase mechanics, and recommended loadouts

Ra's al Ghul — Prologue: Infiltration

League of Shadows · Himalayan Temple
Phases2 Phases (Temple Duel → Temple Escape Brawl)
RecommendedMax-level Batarang, Focus Boost, Perfect Counter
Phase 1Ra's vanishes into smoke, reappearing with shuriken throws or spinning blade charges. Unlike standard ninjas, Ra's can redirect mid-spin — dodge multiple times, not just once. Best opening: dodge his shuriken volley then immediately fire three Batarangs — nearly always staggers him for a combo chain.
Phase 2The temple collapses. Ra's returns with eight ninjas (blade + shuriken types) while rocks fall from the ceiling. Do not waste time clearing adds — he summons fresh ones constantly. Build combo and Focus on ninja adds, then Focus Takedown Ra's. Watch for his 5-shuriken fan attack — roll, don't run.

Bane — Arkham (Chapter 5) & Bane Tower (Chapter 6)

Underground Chemical Workshop / Wayne (Bane) Tower
Core MechanicNormal melee deals zero damage. You must destroy the Venom tubes on his back — this is a puzzle fight, not a DPS race.
RecommendedBatman Power Suit, Robin Hazard Suit, Explosive Gel
Phase 1 (100%–66%)Stand in front of one of the three electrified generators at the arena edges. Watch for his chest-pounding animation — this signals a Venom Charge. Do not dodge early — wait for the red targeting line to flash twice, then side-roll. After Bane crashes into the generator, he is stunned for ~4.5 seconds. Immediately aim a Batarang and sever the green-glowing valve on his back.
Phase 2 (66%–33%)Bane floods the outer ring with green venom (0.5 hearts/sec). Switch to Robin's Hazard Suit (immunity). Smash 4 green chemical barrels, use jump-bricks to assemble a Venom Cannon. Wait for Bane's Debris Throw animation, then fire the cannon directly into his face. Batman grapple-kicks in and rips out the second tube.
Phase 3 (33%–0%)Bane unleashes a triple shockwave ground slam from the room's center. Double-jump the instant the green ripple touches your character's shadow — this grants 8 frames of invincibility. Switch to Batman Power Suit, place Explosive Gel on glowing silver floor panels, lure Bane onto them, detonate to launch him into the final QTE sequence.

Joker — Gotham Parade (Chapter 2)

Downtown Gotham · Thanksgiving Parade Route
FlowBalloon Destruction Puzzle (3 Parade Floats) → Mixed Enemy Waves → Aerial QTE Missile Reflect → Final Melee QTE
StrategyEach float requires Catwoman + Batman cooperation. After the third float, fight melee, pistol, RPG, shield, and electro-baton enemies. Build the inflatable Bat-Balloon, enter the aerial QTE phase — reflect missiles back at Joker. Final QTE chain: tear off trick gun → remove glasses → grapple ankle. This is a spectacle fight. Enjoy it.

Mr. Freeze — Gotham Observatory (Chapter 4)

Gotham Observatory · Cryo-Lab
StrategyFreeze's frost blasts leave persistent slow zones on the ground. Use Glide Suit for air superiority to avoid ground AoE. Destroy four coolant pipes around the arena to weaken his ice armor. His only vulnerability window is during coolant recharge — Batarang stagger, then unload damage. His freeze beam locks your character for 2 seconds — character-swap invincibility frames can dodge it. Fire-based weapons (Flame Batarangs, Explosive Gel) deal bonus damage.

Two-Face — Haly's Circus (Chapter 3)

Abandoned Haly's Circus · Big Top Arena
StrategyTwo-Face flips his coin to determine attack pattern — heads = SMG sweep (take cover behind circus props), tails = baseball bat charge (counterable with Perfect Counter). The arena's rotating stage mechanic shifts the terrain — destroy the four corner consoles to lock it. When he yells "Let's leave it to chance!" throw a smoke pellet — his coin toss will miss, giving you a free damage window. His scarred-side attacks are more aggressive; circle to the unscarred side for safer openings.

Poison Ivy — Botanical Gardens (Chapter 3)

Gotham Botanical Gardens · Greenhouse
StrategyFire weapons deal bonus damage to all plant-type enemies — equip Flame Batarangs and Explosive Gel. Ivy summons Snapdragons (grab attack, QTE escape) and toxic spore clouds (persistent damage zones). When she roots to regenerate, attack the roots with Power Suit's Explosive Gel. Four flamethrower stations in the corners can be activated to clear spore clouds. Phase 2: defeat her giant Snapdragon plant monster.
