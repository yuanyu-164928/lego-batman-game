Collectibles Guide

Four collectible types + all Cosmetic Red Brick locations

🧱

Red Bricks (13 Total)

Cosmetic Red Bricks (8): Unlock alternate suit color schemes — Ninja, Nautical, Filthy Rich, Toxic Waste, Feline, Gothic, Construction, Beauty. Stud Multiplier Red Bricks (5): x2, x4, x6, x8, x10 — multiply together. All five active = one Blue Stud (base 1,000) becomes 3,840,000 studs.

🏆

Batcave Trophies (70)

Spread across 14 collectible-bearing missions, 5 themed trophies each: Bonsai Trees, Surfboards, Disco Balls, Ace Cards, Cat Heads, Rubber Ducks, Boomboxes, Cakes, Coins, Kites, Venus Fly Traps, Spaceships, Snow Globes, League Banners. Completed trophies spawn Stud Fountains in the hub.

🔧

WayneTech Caches (14 Sets)

5 caches each across roughly 7 missions. Used at the Workbench to upgrade gear — Batarang range, grapple speed, Explosive Gel radius, smoke pellet duration. Usually hidden behind skill-gated areas (Catwoman's wall climb, Gordon's gum gun, Batman's grapple). Replay missions via the Batcomputer to grab missed caches.

🚗

Minikit Vehicle Models

Replacing traditional Minikits, completing a 10-piece vehicle model spawns a permanent Stud Fountain in the Batcave or Watchtower hub. Fast-traveling between hubs infinitely refreshes the fountains. 20+ vehicle models to complete across all missions.

💰

Infinite Stud Cache (Free)

Pause Menu → Extras → LEGO Group Online Survey. Complete a 2-3 minute mobile survey, enter the 8-digit code. After restarting, a Stud Cache appears near the Batmobile in the garage. Opens for roughly 20,000–30,000 studs (scales with Exploration upgrades). Refreshes every time you re-enter the Batcave. Fast-travel out and back for infinite farming — this is your zero-cost early-game engine.

🔄

Free Play Replay Method

After beating the game, replay Joker levels in Free Play using Superman (flight skips platforms, heat vision melts gold) and Cyborg (Giant Suit for fast AoE silver-object destruction). Use big-fig characters like Bane or Killer Croc — a single jump-slam destroys up to 15 objects, skipping Stud spawn animation delays. Skip all cutscenes. Studs are collected instantly as you destroy.

🦇

Batwing Street Sweep

Summon the Batwing (Classic 1989) — dual lock-on missiles auto-target destructible objects. Fly from The Narrows to Diamond District holding down fire. Turn around and fly back — the previous zone will have unloaded and respawned all lampposts, benches, and fire hydrants. Ideal for passive farming. Gordon's Rebound Launcher with the Splitter mod is the best ground alternative in dense environments.

Golden Rule: Do not spend a single Stud on character skins or cosmetics before acquiring the x2 multiplier Red Brick. Every Stud spent on a skin delays your real earning power by hours. Prioritize multipliers — the game economy begins to shift at x2 and completely breaks open at x6+.

Essential Beginner Tips

The 10 most important rules for your first 3 hours

1

Prioritize Exploration Skills

Hyper Combo, Stud Magnet (1 & 2), Stealthy Studs, Stud Combo Duration, Speedy Stud Combo — these six skills maximize your Stud income from Chapter 1 onward.

2

Focus is Everything

The "stagger-combo" loop: fire 3 Batarangs to stun → chain attacks to rapidly fill the Focus meter. Focus Takedown instantly eliminates shield, electro, and heavy enemies, or deals massive damage to bosses.

3

Suit Switching is Not Optional

Power Suit = super armor + gel puzzles. Hazard Suit = toxic/chemical immunity (Bane Phase 2). Sensor Suit = stealth + extended dodge. Glide Suit = air superiority. Using one suit for an entire fight is the fastest way to lose.

4

Expand the Batcave Immediately

Batcave expansion terminals (wrench icon when scanned) are not decorative. Each of the 5 expansions reveals hidden areas containing Minikit vehicle models and 10 WayneTech Chips.

5

Claim Your Free Stud Cache

3 minutes: Pause → Extras → LEGO Group Online Survey. Complete it, redeem the code. The Stud Cache refreshes on every Batcave visit. This alone can fund your first multiplier.

6

Dark Knight Difficulty: New Rules

Enemies abandon turn-taking — they all attack at once. Attack startups are roughly 30% faster. Eliminate ranged enemies first, then melee. Getting overwhelmed? Grapple to a high point to reset aggro. Sensor Suit's value skyrockets here.

7

x2 Multiplier Before All Cosmetics

Stud multipliers are multiplicative (x2 + x4 = x8, not x6). Buying Nightwing or a gold suit before x2 delays your money engine by hours.

8

Gordon's Rebound Launcher is a Goldmine

Upgrade with the Splitter mod at any Workbench. His homing rubber rounds auto-track destructible objects in dense environments — the best early-game farming tool before you have multipliers.

9

Minikit Models = Passive Stud Fountains

Completing any 10-piece vehicle model spawns a permanent Stud Fountain in the hub. Fast-travel between the Batcave and Watchtower to trigger infinite respawns.

10

Spend All Materials Before Bosses

Use every upgrade material at the Workbench before each boss fight. Prioritize your primary weapon and Batarang — concentrated investment always beats spreading thin.
