Prologue Batman: Year One 4 Missions

▸
MissionsWayne Manor Grounds (Tutorial) → Falcone Confrontation (Combat Tutorial) → The League of Shadows (Training) → Infiltration (Ra's al Ghul Boss Fight)
CollectiblesInfiltration: 1 Red Brick (Ninja) + 5 Bonsai Trees
New AbilitiesGrapple, Batarang, Stealth Takedown, Focus Takedown
Estimated Time~1.5–2 hours

Story Highlights

  1. Wayne Manor Grounds: Pure tutorial, but dense with environmental storytelling — young Bruce collects homemade costume pieces (cape, goggles, cowl), foreshadowing the theatrics to come. The Gray Ghost poster on young Bruce's wall is a deep TAS reference.
  2. Falcone Confrontation: Bruce gets badly beaten — this is the Batman Begins beat: rage without discipline is useless. The combat tutorial is diagetic — Bruce literally learns he needs training.
  3. The League of Shadows: Snow mountain climbing with Talia, combat training, stealth training, gadget training. Bruce meets Ra's al Ghul, absorbing the League's discipline while rejecting their extremism.
  4. Infiltration: Bruce discovers Ra's al Ghul's true plan for Gotham, refuses to execute a prisoner (the defining moral moment), two-phase boss fight against Ra's in a collapsing temple. He escapes and returns to Gotham — Batman is born.

Chapter 1 The Rise of the Bat Boss: Carmine Falcone New Ally: Jim Gordon

▸
MissionsNew Beginnings → Tricorner Investigation → Riddler → Carmine Falcone (Iceberg Lounge) → Wayne Industries (5 sub-missions) → The Red Hood Gang (Ace Chemicals)
CollectiblesNew Beginnings: Red Brick (Nautical) + 5 Surfboards; Falcone: Red Brick (Filthy Rich) + 5 Disco Balls + 5 WayneTech Caches; Red Hood Gang: Red Brick (Toxic Waste) + 5 Ace Cards + 5 WayneTech Caches
Key DetailIceberg Lounge vault code: 596
StorylineBruce discovers the Batcave, builds the first Batmobile, allies with Gordon, dismantles Falcone's operation, and inadvertently creates the Joker at Ace Chemicals. The Jack Napier-Wayne murder connection — a Burton-esque thread woven throughout the entire game.

Chapter 2 The Cat and the Bat Boss: Joker New Ally: Catwoman

▸
MissionsMimes (Shreck's Department Store) → The Penguin (Arctic World) → Flugelheim → Joker (Parade)
CollectiblesMimes: Red Brick (Feline) + 5 Cat Heads + 5 WayneTech Caches; Penguin: Red Brick (Gothic) + 5 Rubber Ducks + 5 WayneTech Caches; Flugelheim: Red Brick + 5 Boomboxes; Joker: Red Brick + 5 Cakes + 5 WayneTech Caches
StorylineCatwoman debuts as a chaotic-neutral force — initially steals Gordon's Batclaw but ultimately becomes an ally. Harvey Dent is injured during the Mimes attack (foreshadowing Two-Face). Joker's parade scheme — Smilex gas on the crowds — is the chapter climax. He is sent to Arkham, but Arkham is where villains fester, not where they are cured.

Chapter 3 The Dynamic Duo Bosses: Two-Face / Kite Man / Poison Ivy New Ally: Robin

▸
MissionsTwo-Face (Haly's Circus) → Kite Man (Power Plant) → Poison Ivy (Botanical Gardens)
CollectiblesTwo-Face: Red Brick + 5 Coins; Kite Man: Red Brick + 5 Kites; Poison Ivy: Red Brick + 5 Venus Fly Traps + 5 WayneTech Caches
StorylineHarvey Dent transforms into Two-Face. Dick Grayson debuts at Haly's Circus — in a family-friendly adaptation, his parents don't die; he simply messes up a performance and decides to join Bruce. This is the turning point: Bruce is no longer a lone avenger; he becomes a mentor. Poison Ivy's plant-type enemies are weak to fire-based weapons.

Chapter 4 The Bat-Family Grows Bosses: Firefly / Mr. Freeze New Allies: Batgirl & Nightwing

▸
MissionsFirefly (Museum) → Batgirl Begins → Mr. Freeze (Observatory) → Nightwing Transition
CollectiblesFirefly: Red Brick + 5 Spaceships + 5 WayneTech Caches; Mr. Freeze: Red Brick + 5 Snow Globes
StorylineBarbara Gordon's Batgirl origin mission runs parallel to the main story — she is not recruited; she chooses to suit up on her own. Dick Grayson evolves from Robin to Nightwing, marking the passage of time and the first sidekick's growth. Mr. Freeze is comedic Arnold-style, but the frozen-wife storyline carries genuine pathos between the ice puns.

Chapter 5 Arkham Falls Boss: Bane (Round 1) New Ally: Talia al Ghul

▸
MissionsTumbler Chase → Arkham Asylum (Seeing is Believing / A Dog Chasing Cars / A Serious House...)
KeyBane is a three-phase puzzle boss — standard melee does nothing. You must destroy the Venom tubes on his back. See the Boss Guide page for details.
StorylineThe Joker's influence pushes Arkham into total chaos. The Tumbler chase is a direct Nolan homage. Talia al Ghul reappears — she has chosen Batman's path over her father's, foreshadowing the League of Shadows' imminent return.

Chapter 6 The League of Shadows Returns Final Boss: Bane Full Bat-Family

▸
MissionsA Shadow Over Gotham → Bane: Round 2 → The Dark Knight Returns
CollectiblesRed Brick + 5 League Banners + 5 WayneTech Caches
Post-Game UnlocksFree Play Mode, Batcomputer mission replay, full character roster, Bat-Mite complete shop, Dark Knight difficulty, all Batcave challenges
StorylineGotham is occupied — Wayne Tower rebranded as "Bane Tower." The full Bat-Family battles through occupied streets, subway tunnels, and the tower itself. The game concludes with an original ending that subverts expected twists. Talia permanently sides with Batman. Legacy complete: Batman is no longer a man, but an idea, a family, a network that transcends any one person.
