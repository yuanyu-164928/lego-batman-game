Game Overview

Everything you need to know before putting on the cape

⚔️

Arkham-Style Combat

For the first time in LEGO history, a freeflow combat system inspired by Rocksteady's Arkham series. Attack combos, counters, Focus Takedowns, and gadget chain attacks replace mindless button-mashing. The Focus meter builds through precise strikes and counters, enabling instant takedowns. Two difficulties: "Caped Crusader" (standard) and "Dark Knight" — enemies no longer take turns attacking; they swarm simultaneously, with attack startups roughly 30% faster.

🥷

Predator Stealth

All-new stealth mechanics: grapple to vantage points, perch on gargoyle-style structures, execute silent takedowns. Detective Mode is a pulse-based AoE scan highlighting interactable objects, hidden routes, and patrol paths. Smoke pellets, remote-controlled Batarangs, and suit switching add tactical depth.

🏙️

Open-World Gotham City

For the first time in a LEGO Batman title, a fully open-world Gotham spanning four islands, with random crime events, Riddler puzzles, and hundreds of collectibles scattered across the city. Glide between skyscrapers, grapple up fire escapes, and summon 20+ vehicles at will. Fast-travel via the Subwayne tunnel system.

🦇

100+ Batsuits & Cosmetics

From classic gray-and-blue to The Dark Knight Returns armor, from Batman Beyond to Arkham Knight — every era of Batman design is represented. Each suit offers different abilities: Power Suit (super armor + Explosive Gel), Sensor Suit (stealth + extended dodge), Glide Suit (extended air time), Hazard Suit (toxic/chemical immunity). Suit switching mid-combat is essential.

👥

7 Playable Heroes

Batman, Robin (Tim Drake), Nightwing (Dick Grayson), Batgirl (Barbara Gordon), Catwoman (Selina Kyle), Jim Gordon, and Talia al Ghul. Fewer than past LEGO games — but depth over breadth: every hero has a full skill tree, unique gadgets, and suit variants. Post-game Free Play also unlocks playable villains.

💿
